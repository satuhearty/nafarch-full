Services

ARCHITECTURE

We are passionate about design & want to make a difference. Our core service is providing Architectural Consultancy Services (ACP) in accordance to the scope as listed under the Scale of Minimum Fees 2010 (SOMF 2010), issued by the Lembaga Arkitek Malaysia (LAM).

INTERIOR

Interior design is an extension to the shell & core of a building. We value intricate details in our design, thus making interior design an extensions of our passion in detailings. Our principle is always simple & easy construction methods but complexed in design.

COMMERCIAL

Having an extensive involvement in commercial projects throughout the years of experience by the Principal, we approach each & every commercial development with a fresh perspective. Taking into consideration lessons from the past to maximize the customer's requirements.

RESIDENTIAL

Housing is THE most essential part of the built environment. It is what makes cities habitable. As the world evolves with rising modern challenges of the world, we intend to look at residential projects design approaches in a more holistic manner. Houses need to sustain man's needs in order for the world to be livable.

MASTERPLAN

What is a building without its external surroundings? We value masterplanning just as much as we value each buildings we involve with. Thorough masterplanning is crucial in ensuring a city stays sustainable in the long run.

SOCIAL

In this 21st century of developing technology, we foresee the social initiative outreach becomes more & more accessible. With that advantage, we look forward to be involved with or curate projects that contributes to the well-being of the society at large.
